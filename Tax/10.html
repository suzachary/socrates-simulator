So taxation is unjust and the government does not have the authority to tax.

On the contary, the government does have such an authority.

Some may claim that people have a right to property and therefore the government may not tax. However, this is absurd.

John Locke, founder of natural rights, states taxes are necessary to preserve the natural way of life.

For example, in order to protect the right to life, the government requires taxes be taken in order to fund the military and police.

Thus, the government indeed has the authority to tax.
