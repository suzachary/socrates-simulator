So taxation is unjust, and the government has the authority to tax, yet such tax is detrimental to society.

However, this cannot be the case. Some things cannot be accomplished effectively merely by free trade. For example, the army of a country which is composed entirely of mercenaries has little incentive to protect a country they do not belong to. This is seen most clearly when the Hessian mercenaries failed to defend an area of British territory from the Patriots.

Thus, it would appear taxation can indeed be beneficial to society, like to conscript troops from the population to create a People's Army.
