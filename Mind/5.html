So both the mind and material can have specific meaning.

However, it is hard to see that this would be the case.

Take for example a picture of a triangle. That triangle has no meaning on its own. Someone might say that it indicates a pyramid, another says it indicates the invisibility cloak from Harry Potter, and another says it indicates the presence of the Illuminati. Whatever the case, the triangle itself has no meaning; rather, the individuals attach meaning to it.

Even words by themselves have no meaning. We are the ones that attach meaning to the splotches of ink on the paper.

Thus, it would seem material cannot have specific meaning. Therefore we reach a contradiction.
